Karen's Cranberry Nut Dessert

A baking tutorial & tribute

You're invited to learn how to bake a special dessert!
My mom discovered this recipe over 20 years ago in one of her favorite magazines - "Taste of Home".
She prepared it countless times for family gatherings, church events, and special occasions.
Everyone raves at the tantilizing combination of tart and sweet in the decadent cake - and, of course, always asks for the recipe!

Last year my mom unexpectedly passed away from cancer.
This tutorial page is a tribute to her love of baking and all the joy she brought to those around her.

Grab an apron, take some notes, and get ready to enjoy something delicous!
