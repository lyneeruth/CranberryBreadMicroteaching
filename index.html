Karen's Cranberry Bread

A baking tutorial & tribute

You're invited to learn how to bake a most sensational dessert! My mom discovered this recipe in one of her favorite magazines ("Taste of Home") over 20 years ago. She made it for family, church events and special occasions and everyone that tried it was amazed at the tantilizing tart and sweet and chewy combination. When my mom suddenly passed from cancer last year we made this dessert for her memorial and wanted to spread our profound joy and memories of her. So come along and take some notes and get ready to enjoy something specially delicous!
